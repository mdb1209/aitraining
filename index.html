Account Tiering

Know Your Accounts, Prioritize Your Time

Not all accounts are equal. Use this tiering system to invest your time where it creates the most impact — and use the tool below to rate any account instantly.

A
Tier A — High Priority
Visit weekly · Call between visits
  • 5+ referrals per month
  • Consistent, high-quality referral pattern
  • Strong physician or DC relationship
  • Multiple referral contacts in account
  • Open to partnership/program building
  • High patient volume or census
B
Tier B — Growth Potential
Visit every 2 weeks · Regular follow-up
  • 2–4 referrals per month
  • Inconsistent or declining volume
  • Relationship is warm but not deep
  • Some barriers (competing agency, etc.)
  • High upside if relationship strengthens
  • Active opportunity to grow share
C
Tier C — Maintenance
Visit monthly · Touch base quarterly
  • 0–1 referrals per month
  • Low patient volume or census
  • Weak or new relationship
  • Heavy competitor penetration
  • Maintain presence, watch for shifts
  • Re-evaluate quarterly
